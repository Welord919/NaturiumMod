General Info:

Naturium is a mod that adds a wide variety of new materials, weapons, structures, systems, and progression paths to Terraria. Much of the early‑game revolves around gathering Barkion’s Bark and Naturium Ore, but there are other ways to progress. You can explore new structures, craft diffrent themed gear, collect Yu-Gi-Oh card packs, fuse cards into stronger forms, and interact with a new NPC who guides you through the mod’s card system.
I will go into detail about specific parts of the mod, so if you want to remain blind you can finish reading now.

Barkion’s Bark
A core early‑game crafting material. It can be crafted at a workbench using Wood and Acorns, and is used in many of the mod’s first‑tier weapons and tools.

Naturium Bars
The backbone of Naturium’s mid‑game crafting. Created using Naturium Ore combined with Crimtane or Demonite, these bars unlock most of the mod’s weapons, armor, and accessories.

Millennium Pieces
You can craft these by exploring the desert and collecting Desert Fossils, Sturdy Fossils, and Gold. Millennium Pieces allow you to craft early‑game Millennium Items and Apophis‑themed weapons.

Plague Chunks & Decay Gear
The Plaguespreader spawns in the evil desert and drops Plague Chunks, which can be crafted into Plague Flasks. These grant the ability to inflict the Decay debuff with melee weapons, but the main use is to be crafted into Decay‑themed weapons, including a bow and the Reaper's Arm.

Card Packs
Enemies and bosses drop a variety of Legend of Blue Eyes White Dragon (LOB) card packs (Common, Rare, Super, Ultra). Opening these packs grants collectible cards that can be used for crafting, fusing, or use them as weapons. Some cards include: Blue Eyes White Dragon, Red Eyes Black Dragon, Dark Magician, and many more. (Will add more cards and a good few don't have card effects currently, will add more in future updates)

Naturium Bags
Bosses also drop Naturium Bags and Naturium Bar Bags, which contain bulk amounts of Naturium Ore or Bars depending on how far into progression you are. These bags help get more Naturium without needing to mine a lot if that is not for you.

Essence Extraction & Fusion Crafting
Cards can be broken down into Essence using the Essence Extractor item while at a Fusion Altar.
Essence is then used to combine cards into stronger and specialized forms.

Archetype System
Weapons and armor pieces are automatically assigned an Archetype based on their name. Most of these are pretty obvious, but experiment if you are not sure.
Equipping the corresponding Archetype Medallion/Item grants powerful bonuses to all items of that archetype.
The Druid NPC sells the first medallion, and additional medallions can be crafted, found in chests, and the like.

Mysterious Grandpa NPC
A new NPC, Mysterious Grandpa, appears once you have a Card Pack (Which should be pretty easy to get)
He offers:
- Quests that guide you through the card system
- Rewards mostly being packs
He serves as the player’s mentor and shop for card‑related mechanics. (The shop will be updated more in the future)

Structures
Using Structure Helper, Naturium adds several new world‑gen structures:
- World Tree with multiple chests, some locked until later progression
- Jungle and Desert Temples containing themed loot
- Living Wood Outcoves scattered throughout the forest underground
- A floating planet above one of the oceans containing late pre‑hardmode treasures
Exploration is heavily rewarded, and many structures contain items tied to the card or archetype systems.
