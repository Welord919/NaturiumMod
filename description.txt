General Info
Naturium is a mod that adds a wide variety of new materials, weapons, structures, systems, and progression paths to Terraria. Much of the early‑game revolves around gathering Barkion’s Bark and Naturium Ore, but there are many alternate ways to progress. You can explore new structures, craft themed gear, collect Yu‑Gi‑Oh card packs, fuse those cards into stronger forms, and interact with a new NPC who guides you through the mod’s card system.
If you want to remain blind, you can stop reading here.

Barkion’s Bark
A core early‑game crafting material. It can be crafted at a workbench using Wood and Acorns, and is used in many of the mod’s first‑tier weapons and tools. It is also an ammo type.

Naturium Bars
The backbone of Naturium’s mid‑game crafting. Created using Naturium Ore combined with Crimtane or Demonite, these bars unlock most of the mod’s weapons, armor, and accessories.

Naturium Bags
Bosses drop Naturium Bags and Naturium Bar Bags, which contain bulk amounts of Naturium Ore or Bars depending on progression. These bags help players who prefer less mining.

Millennium Pieces
Crafted using Desert Fossils, Sturdy Fossils, and Gold. These allow you to create early‑game Millennium Items and Apophis‑themed weapons.

Plague Chunks & Decay Gear
The Plaguespreader and Plague Worm spawn in the evil desert and drops Plague Chunks. These can be crafted into Plague Flasks, which allow melee weapons to inflict the Decay debuff. They are also used to craft Decay‑themed weapons such as the Decay Bow.

Card Packs
Enemies and bosses drop a variety of Legend of Blue Eyes White Dragon (LOB) card packs (Common, Rare, Super, Ultra). Opening these packs grants collectible cards used for crafting, fusing, or even as weapons. Cards include classics like Blue‑Eyes White Dragon, Red‑Eyes Black Dragon, Dark Magician, and many more.
About half of the cards have effects, but more will be added in future updates.

PSA Grading System
Naturium introduces a unique PSA‑style card grading system.
Players can obtain PSA Cases, which allow them to encase their cards in protective shells and assign them a grade from PSA 0.0 to PSA 10.
Higher‑graded cards:
- Sell for more
- Look visually distinct
- Allows a zoom feature to look at the card
This system adds collection depth for people who like to collect the cards.

Essence Extraction & Fusion Crafting
Cards can be broken down into Essence using the Essence Extractor while at a Fusion Altar. Essence is then used to combine cards into stronger and more specialized forms.

Archetype System
Weapons and armor pieces are automatically assigned an Archetype based on their name. Equipping the corresponding Archetype Medallion grants powerful bonuses to all items of that archetype.
The Druid NPC sells the Barkion medallion, but more can be crafted or found.

Ice Barrier Archetype
Equipping the Ice Barrier Medallion boosts all Ice‑type weapons and projectiles. There are is an armor set, magic weapon, and whip for it curretly.

Shark Archetype
A few ranged weapons and a melee weapon that relate to sharks and the ocean. Has 3 upgraded versions of the Minishark that boost diffrent aspects of the weapon.

Mysterious Grandpa NPC
A new NPC who appears once you obtain a Card Pack.
He offers:
- Quests that guide you through the card system
- Rewards (mostly packs)
- A shop with card‑related items (expanded in future updates)
He serves as the player’s mentor for all card mechanics.

Structures
Naturium adds several new world‑gen structures using Structure Helper:
- World Tree with multiple chests, some locked until later progression
- Jungle and Desert Temples with themed loot
- Living Wood Outcoves scattered throughout the forest underground
- A floating planet above one of the oceans containing late pre‑hardmode treasures
- Crashed Boat — a new ocean structure submerged beneath an ocean
The structures contain items that may be tied to the card or archetype systems for an early boost.

Quality of Life
Naturium includes several QoL items, the most important being the Smith’s Charm and Platform Creators.
- Platform Creators allow rapid placement of platforms with multiple upgrade tiers and a custom UI.
- Charm Pieces that give buffs grouped into 5 diffrent categories.
- Smith’s Charm combines multiple charm pieces into a single accessory, reducing buff clutter.
