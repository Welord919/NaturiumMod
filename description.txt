General Info:

Naturium is a content mod made that adds lots of new content to the game. Some of the new content includes: new ores, a set of armor, weapons, structures, and 2 enemies. The majority of the items in the early game rely on Barkion's Bark and Naturium Ore. You can choose to check out the desert for the custom structure and collect resources to craft Millennium Pieces, which can let you get some good early game weapons and accessories. Most of the content in this mod ranges from early game to early hard-mode. More content is planned for Naturium including a boss, many armor sets, many new structures, and of course tons of new weapons.

I will go into detail about specific parts of the mod, so if you want to remain blind you can finish reading now.

Primary Materials:

Barkion's Bark is one of the primary materials used in most of the early-game item recipes. You can craft Barkion's Bark at a crafting bench using wood and acorns.

Naturium Bars are used in the vast majority of recipes in Naturium past the early-game. You can craft Naturium bars with crimtane or demonite, and of course Naturium Ore.

Millennium Pieces are made with Desert Fossil, Sturdy Fossil, and a bit of Gold to allow the crafting of a few Millennium Items as well as Apophis weapons. The Plaguespreader will spwan in the evil desert and drop unique items, most import of which is the Plague chunks. These will craft Plague Flasks which give you a buff to inflict the decay debuff. These Plague Flasks can further be crafted into Decay items, including a bow and a magic weapon. 

Archetype System:

The Archetype system uses the name of a piece of armor or a weapon to classify it into an archetype. This classification allows the item to receive buffs when the player has the respective medallion equipped. The Druid offers the first obtainable medallion in her shop, but you can just craft it with Naturium at an anvil.

Structures:

With the help of Structure Helper, there are multiple structures that spawn around the world. You can find a World Tree with chests to loot and some you can unlock later. Temple-like structures found in the Jungle and Desert. And multiple living wood out coves all throughout the forest biome's underground.
Make sure to look above one of the oceans for a roaming planet with late pre-hardmode goodies.
