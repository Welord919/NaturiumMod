Naturium is a large content mod that expands Terraria with hundreds of new items, new progression paths, new structures, and a full Yu‑Gi‑Oh‑inspired card system. The mod adds new materials, weapons, accessories, archetypes, world‑gen, and multiple new crafting systems while staying compatible with major content mods and multiplayer. 

Major Features
Hundreds of new items across all stages of the game

New materials & crafting paths including Barkion’s Bark, Naturium Bars, and Millennium Pieces

Yu-Gi-Oh-style card system with card packs, card damage, fusion crafting, and PSA grading

Archetype system that empowers themed weapons and armor

New world-gen structures with unique loot and early-game boosts

Quality-of-life tools like Platform Creators and charm-based buff systems

Mysterious Grandpa NPC with quests, progression, and a card-focused shop
